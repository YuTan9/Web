Tim Duncan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Timothy Theodore Duncan (born April 25, 1976)[1] is an American retired professional basketball player. He played his entire 19-year career with the San Antonio Spurs of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Widely considered to be the greatest power forward of all time,[2] he is a five-time NBA champion, two-time NBA MVP, three-time NBA Finals MVP, and an NBA All-Star Game MVP. He is also a 15-time NBA All-Star[3] and the only player to be selected to both the All-NBA and All-Defensive Teams for 13 consecutive seasons.[4] Many refer to Duncan as the greatest Spurs player of all time.
